### here are some of my highlighted Projects 
### 1. UK Train Operations Performance Dashboard
https://github.com/GaberAlaa/Yala_Dashboard_Day7

Created an operational and financial oversight tool to track $700K+ in revenue and evaluate service efficiency across major UK rail stations.
-	Key Insights: Diagnosed weather issues as the primary driver of revenue loss (Refund Leak); identified London Kings Cross as the highest revenue generator and Manchester Piccadilly as the station with peak delays (60 min).
-	Technical Highlights: Designed a multi-page feel using KPI cards for executive summaries and detailed bar charts for delay-reason root cause analysis.

### 2. HR Employee Analytics Dashboard

https://github.com/GaberAlaa/Yala_Dashboard_Day3

Developed a comprehensive workforce dashboard to monitor employee demographics, compensation equity, and satisfaction levels for 1,470 staff members.
-	Key Insights: Identified salary disparities across education levels and gender; tracked a 10-year trend in employee satisfaction to pinpoint historical shifts in organizational morale.
-	Technical Highlights: Utilized stacked bar charts for compensation benchmarking and line-area charts to visualize sentiment volatility over a decade.

### 3. Global Mortality Rate Analysis Dashboard

https://github.com/GaberAlaa/Yala_Dashboard_Day5

Visualized historical global mortality trends (1970 – 2010) to analyze the correlation between population growth and death rates.
-	Key Insights: Highlighted an inverse relationship between rising global populations and declining mortality rates; identified the 80+ age group and infants under 1 year as the highest-risk demographics.
-	Technical Highlights: Integrated time-series filtering and geospatial bubble maps to provide multi-dimensional analysis across continents.
